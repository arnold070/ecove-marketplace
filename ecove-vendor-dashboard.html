📋 Ecove Marketplace Vendor Policies
1. Product Listing Rules
Only list products you own or have authority to sell. All products must be accurately described. Counterfeit or prohibited goods will result in immediate account suspension.
2. Product Approval
Every product must be reviewed and approved by an Ecove admin before going live. Approval typically takes 12–24 hours. Rejected products include a reason; you may edit and resubmit.
3. Commission Structure
Ecove charges a category-based commission on every sale. Your default rate is 8% (Electronics). Commission rates are set by Ecove and may be adjusted with 30 days notice.
4. Order Fulfillment
You must fulfill orders within your stated handling time. Failure to ship on time may result in automatic cancellation and negative impact on your seller rating.
5. Payouts
Earnings are cleared 7 days after delivery confirmation. Minimum withdrawal is ₦5,000. All payout requests require admin approval and are typically processed within 2–5 business days.
6. Prohibited Items
Weapons, counterfeit goods, illegal substances, adult content, and items that violate Nigerian law are strictly prohibited. Violation results in permanent account termination.
7. Suspension & Termination
Ecove reserves the right to suspend or terminate any vendor account at any time for policy violations, fraud, or poor performance (rating below 3.5).
